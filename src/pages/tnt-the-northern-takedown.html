HARD win the
TNT - The Northern Takedown tournament

Over the 4/5th May 2019 HARD were proud to take part in The Northern Takedown tournament in Denmark hosted by Aalborg Roller Derby.

The five team tournament took place over two days with HARD playing 3 bouts on the first day and one bout on the final day. A punishing schedule for any league but with only 9 players on the HARD roster this was going to be tough weekend.

Aiming for their first international win they exceded all expectations by winning all four games!
